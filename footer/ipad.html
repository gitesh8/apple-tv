Watch on Apple TV (4K, HD, and 3rd generation)

Apple TV+ and other channels are available in the Apple TV app on devices with the latest versions of iOS and iPadOS. Best of all, you can start watching on a mobile device, then finish later in the Apple TV app on a big-screen TV.

iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch

+
1.Open the Apple TV app TV app on your iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch.
2.On the Watch Now screen, scroll down to view Apple TV+, Apple TV channels, or featured movies and TV shows, then select an item.
3.Tap any of the following screens:
  • Watch Now: Start watching movies, TV shows, and sports in one place. Use the Up Next row to find items you’ve added or content you’ve started watching but haven’t finished.
  • Apple TV+: Browse and watch Apple TV+, a subscription streaming service featuring Apple Originals—award-winning films, series, compelling dramas, groundbreaking documentaries, kids’ entertainment, comedies, and more—with new items added every month.
  • Store: Use this all-in-one destination to browse, buy, and rent the world’s best movies and TV shows.
  • Sports: Get immediate access to a wide array of live and scheduled sports events.
  • Library: Find your purchases and rentals, organized by category.
  • Search: Find TV shows and movies by title, cast, or crew.

If you dont't have the latest version of iOS or iPadOS

+
Do either of the following to watch Apple TV+:
  • Update your device to the latest version of iOS or iPadOS
  • Go to tv.apple.com to subscribe to and watch Apple TV+ in a web browser.
    • Apple TV+ requires a subscription. Not all features and content are available in all countries or regions, or on all streaming devices.
